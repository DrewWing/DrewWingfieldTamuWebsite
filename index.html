EDrewcated Guesser

Software I wrote to predict matches for the FIRST Tech Challenge competition.
Open-sourced on GitHub.

  • Loading... Commits
  • 14 Tracked Files
  • 90% Prediction Accuracy
  • 1 Required Device
  • 34,000+ Matches of training data

I developed the EDrewcated Guesser to predict match outcomes for FIRST Tech Challenge team #12928, the LightSaders. The project started small in 2023, but has since grown to be large and open-source. The EDrewcated Guesser grabs current and historical team and match data from the official FIRST API, calculates useful statistics such as OPR and CCWM, predicts match outcomes for a given competition event, and pushes all of the data to a Google Sheets spreadsheet. It comes with two pre-trained Predictive Machine Learning Models, and a few scripts to train new ones.
